About Us

Hi5 Education Centre is a professional learning centre dedicated to supporting learners, teachers, and parents with quality educational resources. We provide organized lesson notes, schemes of work, textbooks, revision materials, and school notices for different education levels.

Our aim is to simplify learning and make education accessible for all learners across Uganda. We also hope to establish a physical school in the future.

Founder: Mr Isa Kiggundu
